Special Projects

Catalane Store Design

This project involved redesigning a Caratlane jewelry store using Blender 3D as part of a retail design assignment. I visited a real store in Budgam, Srinagar, India. to take dimensions, analyze planning issues, and then created a new layout focused on better customer flow and display efficiency.

Using 3D modeling, texturing, lighting, and animation, I recreated the store with a clean, modern look.

Chandigarh Marathon logo Design

I created the official logo design for Chandigarh Marathon, inspired by the city’s iconic modernist architecture and its identity as a green, sustainable city. The geometric form and green-gray palette reflect both the urban structure and the eco-conscious spirit of the event. 

The branding was developed to be bold, versatile, and memorable across apparel and promotional materials.

Boss Bitch - magazine

Created as part of a college assignment, Boss Bitch Magazine is a bold editorial piece inspired by women empowerment. I led the creative direction, from studio photography and fashion styling to layout design and magazine production, showcasing strong, confident, modern independent women.

'CRS' Website Design

As part of my internship at Bulb Studio, I led the UI/UX redesign of Convouyer Ropeways Services Pvt. Ltd.’s official website. I was responsible for user research, wireframing, high-fidelity UI design, and structuring the overall user experience to enhance clarity and engagement.

Mrittika Branding

I designed a complete Visual identity for Mrittika, a hypothetical architectural consultancy, as a part of our assignment at NIFT. This included logo design, color palette, packaging elements, social media branding, virtual Store design for a complete brand identity.

 The design reflects the nature and authenticity of the Architecture studio while offering a fresh, cultural appeal.

NOLAN - Magazine

Created as part of an assignment at NIFT, this magazine explores Christopher Nolan’s iconic films through a blend of editorial layout, graphics, and illustration. I researched his work and designed every element—from visual storytelling to typographic hierarchy—to reflect his cinematography style.

Super Roti Branding

I designed a complete brand identity for Super Roti, a local Kashmiri bakery, to enhance its visual presence and customer recognition. This included logo design, color palette, packaging elements, and social media branding for a consistent and engaging experience across platforms. The design reflects the warmth and authenticity of the bakery while offering a fresh, energized appeal.
